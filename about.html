About Us

HappyLand holiday resort in Pretoria is an authentic portrayal of a turn-of-the-century mining town hotel located within Africa’s largest theme park. Guests enjoy a choice of 70 luxurious en-suite rooms, access to the hotel’s private pools and gardens, and free access to the popular happyLand entertainments . A host of family-friendly dining options are available, from Barney’s Restaurant and Bar to several well-known franchise restaurants, all located within the precinct. Several function rooms and conferencing facilities are available
